Regional Integration. Regional integration will allow countries to bridge energy gaps using external
linkages, including regional electricity integration and trade in hydrogen, biomass, and other fuels.
Countries with greater integration can rely more heavily on imports and may therefore rely less on
indigenous resources (Box 6.10).
