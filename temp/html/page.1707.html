10.4 Decarbonisation of land - based transport

10.4.1 Light - duty vehicles for passenger transport LDVs represent the main mode of transport for private citizens (ITF 2019) and currently represent the largest share of transport emissions globally (IEA 2019d) . Currently, powertrains depending on gasoline and diesel fuels remain the dominant technology in the LDV segment (IEA 2019d) . HEVs , and fully battery electric vehicles (BEVs), however, have become increasingly popular in recent years (IEA 2021a) . Correspondingly, the number of life cycle assessment (LCA) studies investigating HEVs, BEVs, and fuel cell vehicles have increased. While historically the focus has been on the tailpipe emission s of LDVs, LCA studies demonstrate the importance of including emissions from the entire vehicle value chain, particularly for alternative powertrain technologies.
