Societal Preferences. Citizens in every country have preferences for certain technological options or mitigation approaches over others that will influence energy system choices. The public generally prefers a future energy system based largely on renewables . Preferences for non - renewable energy differ across regions and groups . For example, studies have found that people in the U.K., Germany, the Netherlands, and Switzerland prefer renewable energy and personal energy efficiency and savings
