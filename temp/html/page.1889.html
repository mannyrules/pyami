Steel production can be divided into primary production based on iron ore and secondary production based on steel scrap. The blast furnace to basic ox ygen furnace ro ute (BF - BOF) is the main primary steel route globally , while the electric arc furnace (EAF) is the preferred pr ocess for the less energy and emissions - intensive melting and alloying of recycled steel scrap. The direct reduced iron (DRI) route is a lesser used route that replaces BFs for reducing iron o re, usually followed by an EAF. In 201 9 , 7 3 % of global crude ste el production was pro duced in BF - BOFs, while 2 6 % was produced in EAFs , a nominal 5.6 % of which is DRI (World Steel Association 2021) .
