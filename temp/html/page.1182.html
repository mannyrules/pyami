7.2.3 CH 4 and N 2 O flux from AFOLU

Trends in atmospheric CH 4 and N 2 O concentrations and the associated sources, including land and land use are discussed in Section s 5.2.2 and 5.2.3 of the IPCC WGI sixth assessment report. Regarding AFOLU, the SRCCL and AR5 (Jia et al. 2019; Smith et al. 2014) identified three global non - CO 2 emissions data sources; EDGAR (Crippa et al. 2021) , FAOSTAT (FAO 2021a ; Tubiello, 2019) and the USA EPA (USEPA 2019) . Methodological differences have been previously discussed (Jia et al. 2019) .
