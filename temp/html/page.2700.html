The higher deployment in 2°C scenarios cannot be explained solely as a result of technology cost dynamics. In IAMs, technology deployment is also governed by system constraints that characterize both scenarios, e.g. the flexibility of the energy system, the availability of storage technologies. From a image.2701.1.72_504.177_609 modelling point of view, implement ing more stringent climate policies to meet the 2°C targets forces models to find solutions, even if costly, to meet those intermittency and flexibility constraints and temperature target constraints.
