There is a lack of consensus about how CCS might alter fossil fuel transitions for limiting li kely

warming to 2°C or below. CCS deployment will increase the shares of fossil fuels associated with

limiting warming, and it can ease the economic transition to a low - carbon energy system (Muratori et

BECCS might become significantly more appealing than fossil CCS even before 2050 (Luderer et al.
