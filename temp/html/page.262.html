The continued rise of global emissions reflects in part the strongly path - dependent na ture of socio - economic systems, which implies a historic tendency to ‘carbon lock - in’ (Unruh 2000) . An interdisciplinary review (Seto et al. 2016) identifies a dozen main components organised into four types, across the relevant Dimensions of Assessment as summarised in Table 1.3 .
