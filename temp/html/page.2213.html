Almost all Parties outlined dome stic mitigation measures as key instruments for achieving mitigation targets in specific priority areas such as energy supply (89%), transport ( 80 %), buildings ( 72 %), industry (39%), agriculture ( 67 %), LULUCF ( 75 %) and waste ( 68 %). Renewable energy generat ion was the most frequently indicated mitigation option (84%), followed by improving energy efficiency of buildings (63%) and multisector energy efficiency improvement (48%); afforestation, reforestation and revegetation (48%); and improving energy efficie ncy of transport (45%) (UNFCCC Secretariat 2021) . Parties often communicated mitigation options related to the circular economy, including reducing waste (29%) and recycling waste (30%) and promoting circular economy (25%) .
