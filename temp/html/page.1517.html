RECC - LED data include only space heating and cooling and water heating in residential buildings. Over the period 1990 - 2019, global CO 2 emissions from buildings increased by 50%. Global indirect CO 2 emissions increased by 92%, driven by the increase of fossil fuels - based electrification, while global direct emissions decreased by 1%.
