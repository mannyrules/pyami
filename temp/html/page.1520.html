9.3.2 Drivers of CO 2 emissions and their climate impact

Building specific drivers of GHG emissions in the four scenarios described above are assessed using an index decomposition analysis with building specific identities and reflecting the three pillars of the SER framework (sufficiency, efficiency, renewables). Broad drivers of GHG emissions such as G DP and population are analysed using a Kaya decomposition in Chapter 2.
